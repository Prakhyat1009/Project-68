The dreams of Seth Anandram Jaipuria took concrete shape and became a reality when the foundation was laid by our Late Prime Minister Smt. Indira Gandhi on 13th September, 1966. The school became fully operational after the inauguration done by our Late President Dr. Fakhruddin Ali Ahmad on 26th October, 1974.
